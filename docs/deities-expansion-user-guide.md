# Lore Master's Deck: Deities Expansion - User Guide

## What is the Deities Expansion?

The **Deities Expansion** (also known as "Master of Gods") adds 60 new cards to the Lore Master's Deck system:

- **25 Deity cards**: Gods, spirits, deities, and cosmic entities
- **35 Expansion variant cards**: Special versions of the 7 core lore types that connect to deities

This expansion introduces a unique **deity-pairing mechanic** that creates compound names and deeper worldbuilding connections.

2. **Mix and Match**: You don't have to use expansion cards for every slot - combine base and expansion cards

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I have to use the Deities Expansion?
A: No! The base Lore Master's Deck works perfectly on its own. The expansion is optional additional content.

### Q: Can I use deity cards without expansion variant cards?
A: Yes! Deity cards work great as standalone inspiration for creating gods, spirits, and cosmic entities in your world.

### Q: What if I draw an expansion card with multiple âšª symbols?
A: You can either:
- Draw multiple deity cards (one per âšª)
- Use the same deity for all âšª symbols
- Mix in non-deity elements (e.g., faction names, character names)

### Q: Can I create a cluster with a deity as the center card?
A: Not in the current implementation, but this is planned for a future enhancement!

### Q: How many deity cards should I use in my world?
A: The guidebook suggests 3-7 major deities for a manageable pantheon, but you can have as many or as few as you like.

### Q: What's the difference between deity primary and secondary cues?
A: Primary cues are roles/domains/titles (REVELER, NAVIGATOR, ARCHITECT). Secondary cues are the deity's actual name (THE LION, THE SERPENT).
